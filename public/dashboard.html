📌 Server Commands Guide

💰 Economy & Jobs

Want to earn money? Follow these steps:

  • Gather crops, ores, or other resources.
  • Use /jobs to join a job and start making money.
  • Sell your items at /warp Market for extra cash.
🌍 Teleportation & Warps

Need to travel quickly? Use these commands:

  • /tpa player-name – Request to teleport to another player (Costs $50).
  • /tpaccept – Accept a teleport request.
  • /warp Market – Go to the marketplace to buy and sell items.

⚠️ Important: Always set a home or remember your base coordinates before teleporting! If you forget, you might not be able to return easily.

🏡 Homes & Respawn Points

Want to return to your base safely? Use these commands:

  • /sethome home-name – Save your current location as a home.
  • /home home-name – Teleport to your saved home (Costs $50).
  • /home – Return to your bed respawn point.
  • /delhome home-name – Delete a saved home.

💡 Tip: If you haven’t set a home, you will respawn at your bed. If you don’t have a bed, you’ll respawn at the world's spawn point.

🏠 Land Claiming & Protection

Want to protect your house from griefers? Claim your land!

  • /l create land-name – Create a protected land claim.
  • /l edit land-name – Edit your land settings.
  • /l claim – Expand your land by claiming more chunks.
  • /l trust player-name – Allow a friend to build in your land.

💡 Tip: If a player is NOT trusted, they cannot break or place blocks in your claim.

Final Reminder: Always set a home before teleporting or leaving your base! This will help you avoid getting lost.
